FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite up to the given amount of members based on the capacity specified when reserving an office space.

What happens if I miss my reservation?

If you are to miss your reservation. Please contact the admin official per office, or go to the "My Bookings" page and remove the reservation you missed.

Is there a limit for how long I can book a specific space?

There is an eight hour limit per day on each office space. One may also reserve a space for up to two weeks if needed.

What if I book the same space as another emeployee?

The reservation will go to the individual whom reserved the specified office space first.

Does COVID-19 affect how offices are run?

Due to COVID-19, all individuals must adhere to covid regualations; which includes physical distance and reservation of all office spaces. For more info, please visit the official CDC website
